Ramadan Study Group 2020

Salaams everyone and Ramadan Kareem!

This website will be used to host resources and scheduling that we can use to study Quranic themes this month. Below you will find a tentative schedule for what we will cover this week. All archived material will be indicated with a link to the detailed document. Supporting materials will also be linked with the corresponding Surah and topics of discussion for that week.

How this will work:

Every week, we will have a Surah that we will be going over and selected Discussion Leaders to drive the conversation. The format will be:
  • 20-30 mins of Surah recap by Discussion Leaders. Everyone else will have done the reading beforehand, so this won't be an ayah-by-ayah review. This recap will be more high-level or larger themes of the Surah
  • 10-15 mins of contributions by everyone else. This can be in the form of complementary hadiths, connections to other Surahs, or different perspectives
  • 10-15 mins of reflections by everyone in the group. The goal of this will be to derive lessons we can learn from the Surahs, as well as 1-2 action items we can implement in our lives the following week.
